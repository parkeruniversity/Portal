How to make a gift to Jenzabar University
 
Jenzabar University appreciates the generous support of it's alumni and friends, whose gifts continue to benefit Jenza College, the graduate school, and each of the university's professional schools. The Campaign for excellence seeks to ensure that the university continues to excel in scholarship, teaching, and service to society. Priorities include endowment and operating funds for essential university needs: financial aid for students, faculty, programs, research, the campus environment, and unrestricted purposes.
 
To make a gift online, we must gather certain information from you. As you move through the "sections" that follow, you will be asked for various items of information that are necessary to process your gift correctly:
  •    a. Identifying information, including address information
  •    b. where you wish your gift to be used
  •    c. your credit card information
This is a secure site so your information will be encrypted. If you leave this site at any time without having indicated that you wish to submit the gift transaction (you are asked whether you wish to CONFIRM & SUBMIT your gift on the last page), nothing will be sent and, therefore, no gift will be recorded.
