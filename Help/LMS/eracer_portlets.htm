e-Racer portlets

e-Racer comes with several portlets unique to this product, many of which are geared toward largely toward students. These are the primary focus of these help pages. The e-Racer portlets available to students include:

·       About This Course

·       All My Courses

·       Attendance

·       Course Information

·       Course Search

·       Course Syllabus

·       Coursemates

·       Coursework

·       Downloadable Version

·       Gradebook

Note that the pages for your courses may include additional portlets not listed here. If you want help with these, see For more information.
