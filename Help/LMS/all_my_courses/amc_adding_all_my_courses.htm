Adding 'All My Courses'

If you don't have easy access to an instance of the All My Courses portlet, try adding the portlet to your My Pages context.

To add the All My Courses portlet to your My Pages context:

1.        Log in to the portal and navigate to your My Pages context. By default, this is the tab at the far right.

2.        Click Edit Page to open a screen labeled Customize page name.

3.        Click Content.

4.        Click Add a portlet to display a list of portlets available for you to add. This list is controlled by your portal administrator.

5.        Select All My Courses.

6.        Scroll to the bottom of the screen and click Add Portlets to page.

7.        If desired, click Layout to arrange the way the portlets display on the page.
