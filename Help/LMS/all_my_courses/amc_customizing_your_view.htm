Customizing your view

Any time you use the All My Courses portlet, you can choose whether to view past, present, or future courses by making the appropriate choices in the Show drop-down list, which is at the top left of the portlet.

Additionally, you can hide or display parts of what the portlet shows by default.

+ To hide or show any term

To hide any term, click the hide icon.

To expand any term, click the expand icon.

+ To hide or show any list or graphic schedule

You can hide or display any list or graphic schedule view:

·       To hide any list or schedule, click the corresponding hide icon.

·       To show any list or schedule, click the corresponding expand icon.

+ To hide or show all graphic schedules for all the academic terms on the screen

To hide all graphic schedules currently displayed on the screen, click the Hide all schedules link.

If you previously hid the schedules and want to show them all again, click the link labeled Show all schedules.

+ To hide or show all course lists for all the academic terms on the screen

To hide all class lists currently displayed on the screen, click the Hide all course lists link. (Note that the graphic schedules will still display.)

If you previously hid the lists and want to show them all again, click the link labeled Show all course lists.
