The Timecard portlet

The Timecard portlet lets you log data about hours that you worked, as well as sick time and vacation time that you took.

To log timecard data:

  1. In the Timecard portlet, click Enter Timecard. The system displays the Timecard Entry screen, which lets you enter details about time worked.

  1. If you need to enter details about sick time, vacation time, and personal time that you took, click the Show Details button. Enter the appropriate details.

  2. If appropriate, use the Next and Previous buttons to view and enter data for days that may not be displayed on the screen.

  3. Click Save.
