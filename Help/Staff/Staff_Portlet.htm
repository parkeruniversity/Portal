The Employee Information portlet

The Employee Information portlet lets you review personal information related to your payroll records.

To display your personal information:

In the employee information portlet, click any of the following, as appropriate:

  • Review your deduction information – To display details about any dependents you have claimed, including each dependent's phone number, relationship to you, date of birth, student status, handicap status, and address (if different from yours).

  • Review your position information – To display the regular amount (i.e., salary per pay period) and year-to-date amount paid for each position name.

  • Review your tax information – To display your filing status, exemptions, year-to-date amount withheld, and year-to-date taxable wages.

  • Review your dependent information – To display details about your dependents.
