Getting Started Overview

The information contained within Getting Started with CRM Staff is intended to be used as a guide to fully explain the functionality delivered with this module.  We recommend that you take the necessary time to read Getting Started with CRM Staff and understand the content to ensure your success with its configuration, installation, and implementation.

Portlets

Staff consists of five portlets:

  • Employee Information

  • GL Account Lookup

  • Item Approval

  • Requisition Entry

  • Timecard

Depending on your role, you may also have the ability to actually administer timecards, define pay periods, create group combinations, identify the current pay period for timecard recording, and upload the timecards to your ERP system for processing.
