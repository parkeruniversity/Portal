Pay Must Pay Courses

The Pay Must Pay Courses screen displays information on all Must Pay Courses that the student is currently registered for.  Term, Course Code, Title and Amount are shown for each course.  To pay for a course, click the Pay checkbox and click the Pay Now button.  This will take the user to the Pay on My Account screen.  When a course has been paid for, it will be moved to the "Confirmed Courses" section of the Add/Drop Courses screen.
