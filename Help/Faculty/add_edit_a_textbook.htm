Add/Edit a Textbook

These two screens (Add a Textbook and Edit a Textbook) allow you to add information about a new textbook and edit information about an existing textbook for a particular course.  This information can be used in the search of new textbooks to add to courses.

Course (code), Term, Title (of course), and Division of the selected course are listed at the top of the screen.

The following fields are available on each screen:

Field

Description

Book Title

Enter the title of the textbook in this required field

Author(s)

Enter the names of the author(s) of the textbook

Price

Enter the amount your school will pay for the textbook

Fee

Enter the amount the student will pay for the textbook

ISBN

Enter the assigned International Standard Book Number (ISBN)

Publisher

Enter the name of the textbook publisher

Description

Enter a description of the textbook

 

 

Functions

  • Add a Textbook

    From the Faculty Course Control portlet:

    • In the Go Directly To drop-down, click Textbooks to open the Textbook Entry screen.

    • Click the Add New Textbook to Assign to Course link.  This link is also available on the Textbook Search screen.

    • The Add a Textbook screen opens.

    • Enter required and optional information.

    • Click the Save button or the Save and Add Another button if you are adding more than one textbook.

    • The Textbook Entry screen displays the textbook(s) added.

  • Edit a Textbook

    From the Faculty Course Control portlet:

    • In the Go Directly To drop-down, click Textbooks to open the Textbook Entry screen.

    • Click the Edit icon () beside the textbook you want to edit.

    • The Edit a Textbook screen opens.

    • Edit information as necessary.  All fields are editable.

    • Click the Save button.

    • The Textbook Entry screen displays the edited textbook information.
