Search Results (Course Facilities)

This Search Results screen displays the course schedules for the term, campus, building(s), and room(s) entered in the search criteria fields on the Course Facilities Search screen.  The information displayed is grouped by Room and sorted by Meets, Dates, and Course.  The table on this screen displays the following information:

Column

Display

Term

Year/term/subterm combination selected in the search criteria

Campus

Campus selected in the search criteria

Position to Room

Drop-down that allows you to go directly to information about the particular room selected

Building

Building or all buildings selected in the search criteria

Room

Room or all rooms selected in the search criteria

Room Capacity

Number of people the room will accommodate

Course

Name of the course and section number (if applicable) assigned to that room

Title

Title of the course assigned to that room

Division

Division in which the course resides

Catalog (CX only)

Catalog with which this course is associated

Days

Days the course meets

Meets

Times of the day the course meets

Dates

Begin and end dates (mm/dd/yyyy) of the course
