My Account Info

The My Account Info portlet allows the student to view their current balance as well as a list of all prior transactions on their account.  This portlet is composed of two parts:

  • My Account Balances

    The student can use My Account Balances to view the current amount due and includes a link to make a payment, if payment is due, as well as access to the Course and Fee Statement.  If the student clicks on the account balance, he can view all transactions against his account.

  • Course and Fee Statement

    Course and Fee Statement generates a printable statement in .pdf format.

  • Pay Must Pay Courses

    If you have Must Pay Courses that have not been paid, this link will be available.  Once the Must Pay Course is paid, the registration status changes from 'R' (reserved) to 'C' (current).

Student will not be able to view their account information while the Business Office runs a recalc on the system.

  • There are no preferences available for this portlet.

  • There are no settings available for this portlet.

  • There are no security operations for this portlet.

 

Functions

  • View Account Balance

    To view the current Account Balance:

    • Enter the My Account Info portlet

    • Click the My Account Balances link to access the My Account Balances screen

  • Generate the Course and Fee Statement

    To view the current Course and Fee Statement:

    • Enter the My Account Info portlet

    • Click the Course and Fee Statement link provided.

    • Click the View My Course and Fee Statment link to generate the Course and Fee Statement Report.

  • Making a Payment

    To Make a Payment:

    • Enter the My Account Info portlet

    • Click the My Account Balances link to access the My Account Balances screen

    • Click the Make a Payment link provided to access the Pay on My Account screen.

    • Enter the amount of the payment in the Amount to Pay text box provided.

    • Click Submit.

  • Printer Friendly Version

    • Open the My Account Info portlet.

    • From any screen, click the Printer Friendly link or the () icon.

    • A new browser window opens and displays the printer friendly version.

    • Click the Send to Printer button.

    • To exit, click the Exit Print Mode link or the () icon.
