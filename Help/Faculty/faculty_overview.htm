Overview

CRM Faculty allows faculty to view their biographical information as well as their course schedule, class lists, advisee information, and facility schedule (locations, buildings, and rooms).  It enables them to authorize courses for certain overrides (if given the permission to do so), enter textbook information for their courses, and enter grades (midterm and/or final) for their students.

The following portlets are available in Faculty:

  • Advisee Roster

  • Course Authorization

  • Faculty Course Control

  • Facility Schedules

  • Grade Entry

  • Advisor Meetings (available as a standalone portlet as well as through Advisee Roster)

The following Student portlets are available via the Advisee Roster portlet (Advisee Details screen) if you are an advisor and have been given permission to access these portlets:

  • Advisor Meetings

  • Course History

  • Course Needs

  • Course Schedule

  • Degree Audit

  • Financial Aid Awards

  • Financial Aid Document Tracking

  • GPA Projection

  • Grade Report

  • Major Exploration/What If

  • Residence Info

  • Student Schedule

  • Unofficial Transcript
