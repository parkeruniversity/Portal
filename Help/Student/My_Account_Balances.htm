My Account Balances

The My Account Balances screen shows the full Accounts Receivable balance for the student, as reported from the ERP.  The balance for each account is a link that takes the user to the View Transactions screen.  Users can also click a link to Make a Payment or generate the Course and Fee Statement.

This statement may not include all transactions to date and may result in a different ending balance than you see on My Account Balances. Please check with the Business Office for additional details.
