Financial Aid Awards

The Financial Aid Awards portlet allows Students or Advisors to view the Financial Aid awards for that student.  

CX/TE/PX/QX Clients: The student can also accept or decline the award package provided by the Financial Aid office.

This screen shows a Description and Total Award for each award, along with the awarded amount and status of the award for each semester for the selected Award Year.

Preferences

  • There are no preferences available for this portlet.

 

Settings

  • There are no settings available for this portlet.

 

Security

  • There are no security operations for this portlet.

 

Functions

  • CX/TE/PX/QX:  Accepting or Declining Aid

    To accept or decline financial aid:

    • Enter the Financial Aid Awards portlet

    • Click the link to View My Financial Aid Awards.  Each award for the current award year will be shown, with information for each semester.

    • Click the Accept/Decline Aid button.  This button will only be available if you are logged in and viewing your own Financial Aid awards.

    • On the Accept/Decline Aid screen, select the Accept or Decline radio button for each award.

    • Click the Next button.

    • On the Confirm Aid Selections screen, review all the information submitted.  If it is correct, click the Submit button.

    • On the Award Change Results screen, the choices you made will display along with their current status.  Click the Continue button to return to the Financial Aid Awards screen.

  • Printer Friendly Version

    • Open the Financial Aid Awards portlet.

    • Click the Printer Friendly link or the () icon.

    • A new browser window opens and displays the printer friendly version.

    • Click the Send to Printer button.

    • To exit, click the Exit Print Mode link or the () icon.
