Faculty Course Control

The Faculty Course Control portlet displays course loads for selected terms and divisions for faculty members.  

For CX Users
In addition to viewing your course load, you may also be able to view courses of other faculty members, depending on the security given to you if you are associated in some way to those courses.

The default screen displays your course list (Course and Title) and allows you to do the following:

  • View details [Course, Title, Catalog (if applicable--currently used only by CX), and Division] on the main screen via the View Details link.

  • View your schedule via the View My Faculty Schedule link.

  • Access Course Details, Class List, Grade Entry (Update Student Grades), and Textbook Entry screens from the Go Directly To drop-down.

  • Open the main screen by clicking the Faculty Course Control link.

The main screen displays your course list and allows you to do the following:

  • Enter different Term and Division search criteria.

  • If you are using CX or TE and if you have access to view course information or enter/update grades for other faculty members, a Faculty drop-down will display as an additional search criteria option.

  • View your schedule and course details via the View My Faculty Schedule link.

  • Access Course Details, Class List, Grade Entry (Update Student Grades), and Textbook Entry screens from the Go Directly To drop-down.
