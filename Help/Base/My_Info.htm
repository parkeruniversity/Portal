Managing My Info

The My Info portlet is different from other portlets. If exists in only place, and when you view it, you see only data specific to you. These details include the following:

·       Information that may be on record for you in your school's ERP system,

·       Details that you have added for viewing by to other users through a My Info pop-up available in different parts of the portal.

The behavior of the My Info portlet, including the tabs that display and the tasks that you have permission to complete, may vary depending on the setup of the portal and on your own permissions. These help pages cover some common tasks you might have permission to complete.
