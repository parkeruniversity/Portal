Adding portlets

To add a portlet to your My Pages:

1.        Log in to the portal and navigate to your My Pages. By default, this is the tab at the far right.

2.        Click Edit Page to open a screen labeled Customize page name.

3.        Click Content.

4.        Click Add a portlet to display a list of portlets available for you to add. This list is controlled by your portal administrator.

5.        Select the portlets that you want to add.

6.        Scroll to the bottom of the screen and click Add Portlets to page.

7.        If desired, click Layout to arrange the way the portlets display on the page.
