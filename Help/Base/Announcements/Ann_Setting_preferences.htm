Setting preferences

To set your personal preferences for how announcements display when you view the portlet, click the wrench icon and select the Preferences tab. You have the choices described below. After you make your selections, be sure to click the Save button at the bottom of the screen.

+ Default Sort

The Default Sort drop-down list determines how items will be ordered in your view of a particular Announcements portlet instance. You can choose any of the following:

·       Title - Alphabetically by title

·       Author - Alphabetically by author

·       Date Sent - Chronologically, with the newest announcements first

+ Show All Collapsed

If you select the Show All Collapsed checkbox, all announcements will be displayed in collapsed view. That is, only the titles will display -- to read the text of the announcements, you'll have to click through.
