Viewing biographical info

Not all users have a Biographical Info tab in their My Info portlet. However, if you do have access to this tab, you can use it to view details on record for you in your school's ERP system, including your name, address, e-mail address, age, and so forth.

You may also be able to submit requests for updates. Generally, any requested updates must be reviewed and approved from within the ERP system by an authorized user before they will be displayed in the portal.

To request a change:

1.        Log in to the portal.

2.        Click Personal Info.

3.        Select the Biographical Info tab. Note that you may not see this tab depending on how your portal user account was created.

4.        To submit requested changes to your basic biographical details, click the pencil icon next to the Name, Address, and Personal Details label.

5.        In the form displayed, make any changes that you think are needed.

6.        Click Submit. This information will be forwarded to your school's ERP system for review. You will not see any changes until your submission is approved.
