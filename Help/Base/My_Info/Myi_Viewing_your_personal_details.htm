Viewing the My Info portlet

To view or edit the personal details saved in your My Info portlet:

1.        Log in to the portal.

2.        Click the Personal Info link to open the My Info portlet, which includes several tabs that represent different types of information. The available tabs may vary depending on your role and permissions. Some of the tabs you see may include the following:

o      Account Info -- For more details on this tab, see Modifying your display name.

o      Password -- For more details, see Managing your password.

o      Photo -- See also Adding a profile photo.

o      Custom Info -- See also Adding custom info.

o      Office Hours -- See also Adding office hours.

o      Biographical Info -- See also Viewing biographical info.

o      Academic Info -- See also Viewing academic info.

o      Privacy Settings -- See also Setting privacy preferences.

3.        Enter or revise any details as appropriate. Note that, in general, anything you add to the Custom Data and Office Hours tabs will be available for other users to view via your My Info pop-up, as will your name as specified on the Account Info tab. Your photo and e-mail address may also be shown, but this depends on your privacy preferences.
