Viewing your own messages

The Forums portlet includes a simple way to display a list of your own posts and replies, including those that are unpublished while awaiting a moderator's approval.

To view a list of your own messages:

1.        If you haven't already, navigate to the appropriate portlet instance.

2.        If you are not looking at the Forum Home view, display the Forum Home by clicking the name of the portlet instance.

3.        In the upper left pane of the Forum Home, click the button labeled My Posts and Replies.
