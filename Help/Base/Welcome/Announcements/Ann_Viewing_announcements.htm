Viewing announcements

To view announcements:

1.        In you haven't already, navigate to an instance of the Announcements portlet. Note that different instances of the portlet may show different announcements.

2.        Click on the Show All Announcements link to maximize the window.

3.        Click on any announcement to expand or collapse its text.

If you have permission to create announcements, note that you can view announcements that you've sent in your Outbox.
