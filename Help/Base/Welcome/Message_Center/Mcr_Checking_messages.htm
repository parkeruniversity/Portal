Checking your mail

To check your Message Center mail:

1.        Navigate to your Message Center, which by default is located in your My Pages context.

2.        The default view shows all messages in your inbox. You can optionally filter out some of the messages by setting the Show drop-down list to one of the following:

o      All Messages

o      Personal Messages

o      Portlet alerts

o      System alerts

3.        Click on the subject line of any message to see its full text. Alternatively, you can click Read All to display the full text of all messages.
