Adding a profile photo

If desired, you can add a profile photo to your account. This photo can then be shown in your My Info pop-up for others to view. The photo may also be shown in other areas -- for example, if you publish posts in Forums, the photo can be shown along with your name next to your posts, and if you are a student, the photo may also be used on your grade sheet. Profile photos should be 133 by 133 pixels and in GIF or JPG format.

+ To add or replace a profile photo

1.        Log in to the portal.

2.        Click Personal Info.

3.        Click the Photo tab.

4.        Use the Browse button to locate the appropriate photo on your local system. Follow the prompts to populate the Add Photo field with the path to the image.

5.        Click Save.

+ To delete a profile photo

1.        Log in to the portal.

2.        Click the Photo tab.

3.        Click Delete Photo.

 

Even if you choose to upload a profile photo, you can also hide it from the My Info pop-up and other parts of the portal. For details, see Setting privacy preferences.
