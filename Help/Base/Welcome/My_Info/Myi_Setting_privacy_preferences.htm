Setting privacy preferences

Throughout the portal, individual users' names -- including your name -- typically are accompanied by a blue My Info icon . Other users can click this icon to display a "My Info pop-up" that shows a little more information about you. The specific details shown in the pop-up come from your My Info portlet. Specifically, the following details from the portlet will display in your My Info pop-up for other users to see:

·       Your name (for more details on this, see Modifying your display name).

·       Any details that you have added on the Custom Info tab.

·       Any details that you have added on the Office Hours tab.

·       Depending on your preference, your profile photo and/or your e-mail address. However, you can hide these, as described below.

+ Hiding or displaying your photo and/or e-mail address

1.        Log in to the portal.

2.        Click the Personal Info link to open the My Info portlet.

3.        Click Privacy Settings. At the top of the screen is a label that says Show the selected items in my My Info popup and in the Constistuent Search portlet. Note that your preference here also affects other parts of the portal besides just My Info and Constituent Search. For example, when you hide your photo from the My Info pop-up, you also hide it from other places in the portal, such as your Forums posts, your grade sheet, and so forth.

4.        Select or de-select E-mail and Photo as appropriate.

5.        Click Save.

The My Info pop-up can be launched from various parts of the portal. For example, if you post an attributed item in a Forums portlet instance, your name and the blue My Info icon will display with the item. Similarly, if you are enrolled in a class, your name and My Info pop-up will be available in the Coursemates portlet, and if you are a member of a Campus Group, your name will show in a group directory portlet. There are other examples as well.

Your name and My Info pop-up may also be available from the Campus Directory, although can hide yourself from the directory, as follows:

+ Hiding or showing your name in the Campus Directory

To hide yourself or add yourself to the Campus Directory.

1.        Log in to the portal.

2.        Click the Personal Info link to open the My Info portlet.

3.        Select or de-select the checkbox labeled Show Me in the Campus Directory.

4.        Click Save.

If the checkbox is grayed out, this probably means that an administrator has chosen to hide you from the directory, and you cannot supersede this.
