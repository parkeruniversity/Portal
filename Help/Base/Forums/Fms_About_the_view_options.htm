About the view options

The portlet gives you the ability to set a few preferences related to how posts and replies are displayed. You do this through the View Options or View/Reply Options dialog box. You have the following choics:

+ The 'Show' option -- should the portlet display the full text of replies, or list them?

Under the Show label, the portal gives you the following options:

·      Post and Replies

·      Single Message

This option controls the view you see after clicking the subject line of a post from within the Forum Home or a similar screen.

If you choose Post and Replies, when you click on the subject line of any post, the system shows the full text of that post, as well as the full text of the first few replies.

If you choose Single Message, when you click the subject line of a post, the system will show the full text of that post only and summarize the replies in a list. Within the list, the system highlights the item whose text you are currently displaying. You can click any item in the list to display the text of that item instead of the original post.

+ The 'Messages per page' option -- how many replies should be represented per page?

You can have the portal display 5, 10, 15, or 20 items per page. You set this using the Messages per page drop-down list.

The default choice is 5. In this case, if you have selected Post and Replies as your Show preference, the system will show the full text of the original post and up to four replies on a single page. If you have selected Single Message as your Show preference, the system will show the original post you selected and then list a maximum of four replies on each page. In both cases, you can page backward or forward to see additional items.

+ The 'Sort' option -- how should replies be indented and ordered?

Under the Sort label, the portlet gives two options: Chronological and Discussion.

If you choose Chronological, then each time you display a post, its replies will be listed in chronological order, and all items will be flush left, regardless of whether they are replies to the original post or replies to other replies.
