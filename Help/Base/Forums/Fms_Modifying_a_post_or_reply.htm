Modifying a post or reply

Depending on your permissions, you may or may not have the ability to edit your own posts and replies.

To modify a post or reply:

1.        Navigate to the post or reply that you want to modify.

2.        Click the Edit link. In response, the system opens the post for editing. (If you do not see the Edit link, that means you do not have permission to edit your item. Note that your permissions may vary between one portlet instance and another.)

3.        Make any changes that are appropriate. If you need help with any field, see About posts and replies.

4.        If desired, click Preview. While looking at the preview, do not click Cancel unless you want to discard the item. (If you want to make changes, click Edit.)

5.        Click Submit to re-publish the message. In response, the system either re-publishes the modified message or displays a message stating that moderator approval is required before your item can be published.
