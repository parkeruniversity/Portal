Group Directory

The Group Directory portlet is a directory of users in a context. Two common examples of the portlet are:

·       Coursemates -- A Coursemates portlet instance lists the instructor for and teachers in a course, and any other people who are affiliated with the class, such as teaching assistants.

·       Groupmates --  A Groupmates portlet instance lists the group leader(s) and members.

The amount of detail for each person will depend on how much information they have chosen to share.
