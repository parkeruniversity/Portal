Viewing posts

This topic explains how to view posts in a Blog portlet instance.

You can also view posts using other methods, such as through e-mail. For more details, see Subscribing to the Blog portlet.

+ About the main portlet view

The initial view of the Blog portlet shows only the most recently posted items. Generally, this view includes between two and 10 posts. Depending on the way your school's portal is set up, the display will include:

·       The full text of these posts.

·       Only a portion of each post; in this case, you can click through to see the full text of each item.

The system uses a vertical bar at the left to show whether a post is new since the last time you viewed the portlet instance. (The bar is either blue or red -- red if the item is marked “important,” blue otherwise.) The next time you view the portlet, the bar will not be displayed, even if you haven't yet clicked through to see the full text of the post.

+ Viewing older items

You can view older posts by scrolling to the bottom of the portlet instance and clicking the Older Items link.

In response, the system displays a page that includes any older posts that exist. This page includes an archive at the left. The archive includes a list of months to which you can navigate, and a drop-down list that lets you display posts from previous years.
