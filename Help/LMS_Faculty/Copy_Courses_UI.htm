Copy Course Materials

You use the Copy Course Materials portlet to copy portlet content from one course context into another.

+ Who has permission to copy, and to where?

Only members of the Faculty and Administrators role have permission to copy courses. Additionally, unless you are a member of the Administrators role, you can only copy to and from your own course contexts.

In other words, only members of the Administrators role can copy one instructor’s materials to another instructor's course.

+ Key terms used in the Copy Course Materials portlet

When you copy, the context that you copy from is called the copied context, or source context. The one you are copying to is called the target context.
