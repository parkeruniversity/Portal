About your course context

Your course context is a series of pages that exist solely for a course section that you teach. It is a place where you can make materials available to students, create assignments, and interact with students.

You can display a course context by expanding the My Courses area of the sidebar and clicking on the name of the course, or by clicking the name of the course in the All My Courses portlet.

These help pages cover certain tasks that you might want to complete relative to a course context, including:

·       Copying materials from one context to another

·       Adding a sub-section to your context

·       Storing materials from a course context in your File Cabinet
