Faculty Guide overview

This section of the help system includes excerpts from e-Racer: Faculty Guide. The full content is available in PDF form from Jenzabar's support site.

+ To check Jenzabar's support site

1.        Open a browser and navigate to MyJenzabar.net/ics.

2.        Log in to the site.

3.        Choose the Support tab, then use the pane at the left-hand side of the screen to navigate to the appropriate page.

If you do not have access to MyJenzabar.net, check with your portal administrator.
