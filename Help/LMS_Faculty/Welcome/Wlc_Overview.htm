Overview

e-Racer is a learning management system that caters to both students and instructors. For example:

·       Faculty members can view course schedules, course descriptions, and class rosters. They can distribute handouts, create and grade assignments, track attendance, submit grades, and much more.

·       Students can view their course schedules, take online tests, check their grades, and review their attendance records. They can also complete online assignments and participate in chat groups and forums.

This help system details a handful of features that may be useful to faculty members. If you need more detailed guidance, or if you want help with different portlets, see For more information.

Note that this product is highly customizable. For example, this guide may describe portlets that are not used by your institution, and your institution may have added its own custom portlets. Additionally, the illustrations in this guide system were created using the default artwork and site design in effect when e-Racer was installed. If your site has been customized with your school’s own logos and artwork, your view may be different.
