Overview

Candidate consists of several portlets designed to help applicants and admissions officers. Candidate offers the following functionality:

  • Potential students can apply for admission.

  • Admissions officers can communicate with applicants.

Portlets

Candidate consists of the following portlets:

  • Academic Information

  • Admissions Status

  • Apply for Admissions

  • Division and Major Assignment (for administrators only)

  • Folder Items

  • Helpful Links

  • Initiate a Contact

  • Make an Inquiry
