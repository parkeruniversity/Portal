The Apply Online portlet

The Apply for Admissions portlet allows you to complete and submit an online application.

If you were allowed to save an incomplete and unsubmitted online application, you will be given an application key.  You must use this application key along with the email address you entered on the incomplete application to retrieve the incomplete application.

Preferences

  • Auto-Fill My Personal Info

    If the institution has your personal information in their database, this setting allows this personal information to automatically populate the form.

    • From the Apply for Admissions portlet, click the wrench icon to open the Portlet Settings and Preferences screen.

    • Select the check box on the Preferences tab.

    • Save.

Functions

  • Apply for Admissions

    • From the Apply for Admissions portlet, click the appropriate link for the type of application that applies to you.

    • In the Select Form screen, click the link of the application form you want to submit.

    • Enter required information (indicated by an asterisk) and other requested information.

    • Click Submit.

    • You may be able to Save and Complete Later if you decide not to submit the form immediately.  An Application Key Number will be assigned to you and displayed on the screen.  This number MUST be used to access the incomplete form so that it can be submitted at a later date.

    • A message verifying your submission displays.

    • Click OK.

    • In the Payment Method screen, select the method by which you want to pay the application fee and click Continue.

    • The Enter Payment Information screen appears for you to complete your transaction.

      • Verify that the payment amount and description are correct.

      • Enter requested credit card information.  All required fields are designated by an asterisk (*).

      • Enter billing address and other requested information for the credit card account you are using.   All required fields are designated by an asterisk (*).

      • Enter your email address so that an electronic receipt can be sent to you.  This field is required.

      • Click Continue.

    • The Verify Payment Information screen appears.  If Information is correct, click Continue.  If information is incorrect, click Back to enter corrections.  You can also Cancel this transaction.

    • The Payment Submission Results screen appears indicating whether your payment submission has been accepted or denied.  If denied, a reason code and description will display.

    • Click Return to Apply for Admissions link.  The form submission status message displays.  Click OK to complete the submission process.
