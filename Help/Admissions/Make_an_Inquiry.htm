Make an Inquiry

The Make an Inquiry portlet allows a Candidate to complete and submit an online inquiry or an Admissions Officer/Counselor to complete and submit an online inquiry on behalf of a Candidate.

Inquiry forms are accessed by clicking the appropriate link on the portlet.  When you click on a link, the Select Form screen displays a list of inquiry forms that are available to you for submission.

Preferences

  • Auto-Fill My Personal Info

    If the institution has your personal information in their database, this setting allows this personal information to automatically populate the form.

    • From the Make an Inquiry portlet, click the wrench icon to open the Portlet Settings and Preferences screen.

    • Select the check box on the Preferences tab.

    • Save.

Functions

  • Make an Inquiry

    • From the Make an Inquiry portlet, click the appropriate link for the type of inquiry that applies to you.

    • In the Select Form screen, click the link of the inquiry form you want to submit.

    • Enter requested information.  The following fields are required for submission:

    • Electronic Mail Address

    • First Name

    • Last Name

    • Click Submit.

    • A message verifying your submission displays.

    • Click OK.
