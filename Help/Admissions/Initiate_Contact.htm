Initiate Contact

The Initiate Contact portlet allows you to request a contact via email or telephone and/or schedule a visit.

Candidates can personally request a contact or schedule a visit.  Admissions Officers and/or Counselors can also enter a contact request and schedule a visit on behalf of a Candidate.

Functions

  • Request a Contact

    Clicking this link allows you to request a phone call or email from Admissions personnel.

    Note:  Your Email Address is a required field.

    When you submit the request, an email is sent to your assigned Administrator, Admissions Administrator, Admissions Officer, and/or Counselor.

  • Schedule a Visit

    Clicking this link allows you to schedule a campus visit.  You can indicate three available dates and times when you would like to make the visit, number of persons accompanying you, with whom you would like to meet, and what you would like to discuss.

    Note:  Your Email Address is a required field.

    When you submit your request, an email is sent to your assigned Administrator, Admissions Administrator, Admissions Officer, and/or Counselor.
