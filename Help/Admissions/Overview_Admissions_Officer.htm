Overview

Admissions Officer allows for management of personnel in the Admissions Office.  It lets:

  • Admissions officers access information about other admissions officers, counselors, and candidates.

  • Counselors access information about candidates assigned to them and to make an inquiry on their behalf.

Portlets

Admissions Officer consists of the following portlets:

  • Admissions Officer and Counselor Assignments (for Administrator only)

  • Admissions Status

  • Division and Major Assignment (for Administrator only)

  • Financial Aid Estimator (CX only)

  • Folder Items

  • Initiate a Contact

  • Make an Inquiry

  • Travel Assistant

  • View Activity

  • View Candidate
