Online Giving

Online Giving allows you to make gifts and pledges to the institution via e-checks and credit cards and to designate how your gift/pledge is to be used.

The step-by-step transaction process is easy to use, and you have the option to target your gift to specific programs.   All gifts and pledges can be given in memory or in honor or an individual.  When making a pledge, you can also indicate how and when the pledge will be fulfilled.

Personal information, which is controlled by privacy settings in My Info, can be viewed and updated by the constituent from within the Online Giving portlet.  

  • If you are not logged in or do not have a login and password and click the Give a Gift or Pledge link, the Personal Information screen displays and requires you to enter biographic information before the Gift or Pledge screen displays.

  • If you are logged in, personal information can be viewed and updated by clicking the Verify personal information link or tab.

Giving opportunities can be categorized in one of five ways:

  • Unrestricted

  • Campaign Funds

  • Designation Funds

  • Financial Aid/Scholarship Funds

  • Memorial/Honor Funds

When a gift or pledge is entered and the Confirm & submit donation link is clicked, a summary is displayed for review along with a final Confirm & submit link, which, when clicked, opens the Payments portlet for your convenience in submitting payment for the gift/pledge.  

     

    Functions

    • Make a Gift or Pledge for Unrestricted Funds

      • Pledge

        From the Online Giving portlet:

        Click the Give a Gift or Pledge link.

        The Gift or Pledge screen opens with the Give a Gift or Pledge tab active.

        For the Unrestricted Funds designation, click the Make a Pledge button.

        The Enter a gift or a pledge tab is activated.

        Complete the required fields, indicated by an *.

        You can optionally enter special instructions/requests.

        If you select one of the Dedication options (In memory of or In honor of), you must enter a name in the Name of Honoree text box.

        If you select the Check to notify someone of this gift and provide name and address below check box, you must enter name and address information in the name and address fields provided.  If you enter name and address information and did not select a Dedication option, you will be required to do that.

        Click the Continue button.

        A summary of your pledge is displayed on the Confirm & submit donation tab.

        Review the summary of your pledge.

        If you want to remove the pledge, click the Remove button to return to the Confirm & submit donation tab on the Gift or Pledge screen.  The message indicates that your gift/donation is $0.

        If you want to edit the pledge before submitting, click the Edit button, edit as desired, review, and then click the Confirm & Submit button.

        If you did not make an initial gift today and want to submit it without changes or after editing, click the Confirm & Submit button.  The Submitted screen indicates the success of the submission.  Click the Continue button to return to the Gift or Pledge screen.

        If you made an initial gift today and want to submit it without changes or after editing, click the Confirm & Submit button:

        The Payment Method screen displays.  The only type of payment available is Credit.  Click the Continue button to display the Enter Payment Information screen.

        Complete required fields, and click the Continue button.

        The Verify Payment Information screen allows you to review your payment information.  If you need to edit the information, click the Edit button.

        Click the Continue button.

        The Payment Submission Results screen displays your transaction information.

        Click the Return to Online Giving link.
