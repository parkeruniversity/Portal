Planned Giving Management

The Planned Giving portlet allows the Advancement Officer to view the different types of planned gifts that are currently being solicited and those that have already been received by your organization.  

Functions

  • View Status of Planned Gifts

    Clicking the View status of planned gifts link displays the Initiative Summary screen, which lists planned gifts that are tracked and managed by the Advancement Officer.  Information may include the type of planned gift, name of the constituent, current status of the gift in the cultivation process, and the amount that is being solicited or has been committed or received.

    A summary of planned giving information displays when a specific constituent link is clicked.  The summary may include the following:

    • Planned gift details

    • Designations or gift categories

    • Changes in status

    • Associated contact details
