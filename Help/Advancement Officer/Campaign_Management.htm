Campaign Management

The Campaign Management portlet provides an analysis of campaigns.

    Functions

    • View Campaign Details and Statistics

      When you clink the link labeled "View campaign details and statistics," the system displays the Campaign Summary screen, which lists campaigns that are tracked and managed by CRM Advancement Officer. Data displayed may include the campaign name, the goal amount, the total amount and percentage of gifts and pledges, and the total amount and percentage of gifts and pledges that have not been received.

      When you click on a specific campaign, the system displays a summary of campaign information, which may include the following:

      • Goal Comparison - Shows progress toward goals.

      • Gift Summary - Shows counts and totals of outright gifts and pledge payments.

      • Pledge Summary - Shows counts, pledge totals, and unpaid totals of complete and incomplete pledges.

      • Designations - Provides a link to details and summarizes totals for pledges, matching gifts, and gifts.

      • Appeal Analysis - Summarizes return, costs, and return on investment.

      • Campaign Staff - For each campaign, displays a list of all staff members reporting to the Campaign Manager, as well as the prospects and donors that have been assigned to each staff member.
