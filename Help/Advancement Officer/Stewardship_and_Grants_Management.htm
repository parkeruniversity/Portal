Stewardship and Grants Management

The Stewardship and Grants Management portlet assists Advancement Officers in decision-making, planning, and analysis of information that conveys appreciation to donors and enhances constituent relationships.  Grant proposals and awards from foundations, corporations, and/or government funding sources can be tracked in detail.

Functions

  • View Major Initiatives

    Clicking the View major initiatives link displays the Initiative Summary screen, which lists initiatives.  Initiative information may include the campaign and goal for each initiative listed.

    Clicking the initiative link displays details and may include the following related data:

    • Associated persons

    • Assigned staff members

    • Related grant proposals and awards

    • Names of recipients of funds generated by the initiative

    • Requisite requirements or preferred characteristics for individuals receiving the funds

    Grant information includes associated persons (patron, recipient, etc.), grant status, proposal activities, and payment information.
